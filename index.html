About

Repr is more than just a tech club. Repr is a community that fosters diversity: our goal is to help our members of all backgrounds and experiences thrive within the tech community.

What?

Repr is a club open to all students within the Bi-Co (Bryn Mawr and Haverford Colleges) with an interest in tech. Whether you are an arts major looking to create a personal website or interested in a career in software engineering, Repr will provide you with the resources and support system that you need.

How?

We hope to host several events throughout the year that are of interest to everyone: career-oriented workshops, diversity panels, as well as general knowledge sessions on topics that you would like to see. We will also have chill weekly study sessions, where you can come to socialize and do cs-related work, be it school work or a personal project.

Who?

Bi-Co students of all years, experiences, and backgrounds are welcome to join Repr. **Additionally, Repr is especially for you if you are a POC or religious minority that is underrepresented in tech. We founded Repr with the intention of being an inclusive resource/support system for minorities.**
